About me

My name is Wendy of Celebrant Services By Wendy
I live in East Derbyshire but are willing to travel around the UK (or further afield) if required.

I trained to be a Celebrant through the ICPC - International College of Professional Celebrants and I am currently undertaking  NOCN level 3 Certificate in Civil Celebrancy in the UK. 
I offer services tailored to the needs of the individual, the couple or the family. Ranging from Traditional Weddings, Vow Renewals and Naming Ceremonies, to End of Life Celebrations with Funerals, Scattering of Ashes and Interment of Ashes.  These can include additional aspects of celebration with Hand Fasting, Unity Sand Ceremonies and Wine Box ceremonies to name a few.
My core values are Professionalism, Respect, Inclusivity, Honesty and Compassion.

Every ceremony I do is a reflection of your hopes and dreams
